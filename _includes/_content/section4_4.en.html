We launched this platform, translated as "Where do my taxes go?", again in June with a new design in order to show State Budget in a clearer and more visual way. We also added a search engine in order to locate detailed expenses without the need to know the precise budget line they belong to, as we added didactic and dissemination information.

One can now find much more and better information and we can dive into the smallest expense item. This is why, even if unique users went down from 51,600 in 2013 to 33,000 in 2014, the average consultation time lasted almost 60% longer.
