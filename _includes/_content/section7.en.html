Awards and acknowledgements

In 2014, the work of Civio foundation was acknowledged with the first position of the journalistic innovation ranking by the communication research group on ('GICOV') of Miguel Hernández University in Elche. Civio, El Confidencial, Vis-à-Vis, eldiario.es and Acuerdo were, according to this ranking, the top five innovative journalistic initiatives for that year. Within the field of journalism, the work of the foundation has also been acknowledged as a “prime example” in The Art and Science of Data-driven Journalism report, by Columbia Journalism School, on data journalism, given that this work was taking place in a country still missing a transparency act.

Moreover, the Spanish members of the Open Knowledge Foundation (OKFN-Spain) selected the Civio foundation as the best non-public initiative for transparency at the first edition of the OKFN prize to Open Knowledge, Open Data and Transparency.
